Percent of County Population with Confirmed Cases

Jul 18, 2020

The map below demonstrates the percent of confirmed cases per county, based upon each county's population.

By mid July, 2020, more than .1 percent of the the population of most U.S. counties had been infected. In many counties, >5% of the county population had experienced infection. Interestingly, Utah appears to be an outliar, with most of its counties still not reporting cases.
